# Claim Boundary Checklist

Status: manuscript guardrail document

## Allowed internal claim

> CGHSTC provides a selective bound-domain coupling framework, and BDVR is its galaxy-sector effective law.

## Allowed APS-facing claim

> Conformal Gravity Hilbert Space Time Current provides a selective bound-domain coupling framework, and BDVR is its galaxy-sector effective response law.

## Allowed scientific claim

- BDVR is presented as a phenomenological galaxy-sector response law.
- The frozen M4c implementation was evaluated on a SPARC quality-1 flat-velocity sample.
- The validation uses global parameters, no per-galaxy Q tuning, and no deletion of the leading outlier.
- Baseline and ablation comparisons show that the halo and baryonic-structure factors change residual behavior relative to simpler models.
- UGC07125 is retained as a verification target, not tuned away.

## Forbidden or premature claims

- Do not claim BDVR replaces dark matter.
- Do not claim the theory proves dark matter is unnecessary.
- Do not claim the model is a full SPARC rotation-curve fit.
- Do not claim an action-level derivation exists.
- Do not claim UGC07125 is explained.
- Do not claim SN 2020qmp changed the global rotation dynamics of UGC07125.
- Do not claim the BEC dark matter paper confirms BDVR.

## Required wording around UGC07125

Use:

> UGC07125 is retained as the leading residual object and defines an external verification target for kinematic integrity, gas participation, HI morphology, and mass-estimate systematics.

Avoid:

> UGC07125 proves the gas-participation hypothesis.

## Required wording around Conformal Gravity Hilbert Space Time Current

For internal notes, `CGHSTC` is acceptable.

For APS-facing text, use `Conformal Gravity Hilbert Space Time Current` at first mention and define any acronym clearly.
